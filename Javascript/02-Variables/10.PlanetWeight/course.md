# 10. Planet Weight

## Congrats!

Woohoo! You learned variables in JavaScript! 🙌

Here's a recap of everything we learned in this chapter:
* `let` and `const` keywords.
* Data types: Numbers, Strings, Boolean, Null, and Undefined.
* Arithmetic operators: `+`, `-`, `*`, `/`.
* `%` modulo operator finds the remainder of division between two numbers.
* `**` exponent operator raises one number to the power of another number.

Let's put it all together one more time!

## Instructions

The year is 2099... we can now take a vacation on the moon or Mars! 🚀

Create a weight conversion program that:
1. Takes your weight on Earth.
2. Converts it to your weight on either the moon or Mars.

To calculate your weight on Mars:

`destination_weight = earth_weight * 0.38`

The output of the program should look like this:
```text
Your weight on Earth is 210 pounds.
Your weight on Mars is 79.8 pounds.

```
